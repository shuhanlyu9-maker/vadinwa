About the Foundation & AMA

The Australian Medical Association (AMA) is the national peak body for doctors and medical students, advocating for high standards, ethics and equitable access.

  • Founded in 1962, representing 30,000+ members
  • Improving access & quality in rural and remote areas
  • VAD Access Fund focus: clinician training, process navigation, advocacy
Australia VAD Legal Timeline (excerpt)
1995 — NT first legislation
1997 — Federal law overturned NT law
2017 — VIC passed law; effective 2019
2019 — WA passed; effective 2021
2021–2023 — Other states progressively effective
2024 — ACT passed; effective 2025-11-03

Note: NT currently has no active VAD law, though rights to legislate have been restored.
